Technique Library

Every technique you've ever drilled, organized by position, searchable by name, and scored for recall.

  • 🔍
    Search & Filter
    Find techniques by name or filter by position (Guard, Mount, Back, Side Control…) and type (Submission, Sweep, Pass…).

    Techniques are grouped by BJJ position with count badges, making it easy to spot gaps in your game.

Everything Included

Built Specifically for BJJ Practitioners

Every feature in MatIQ was designed around how grapplers actually think about their training.

📵
Fully Offline
No account, no login, no server. All your data lives on your device via local storage.
🔁
Spaced Repetition
Technique recall scoring uses review history to surface techniques before you forget them.
📲
Haptic Feedback
Subtle vibrations on interactions make the app feel native and tactile on mobile devices.
🎯
Belt Time Goals
Realistic mat-time targets for each belt level, automatically calculated from your session history.
🌙
Dark Mode First
Deep navy theme designed for gym environments where bright screens are a distraction.
🏅
Achievement System
10 unlockable badges reward consistency, volume, and competition to keep you motivated.
