4
Socratic Verification

Generate Verification Questions

If a submission is flagged as high-risk, use the Verification tab to generate personalized Socratic questions. EduShield sends the text to Anthropic's API to produce questions a genuine author could answer but an AI could not easily fabricate.

★ Requires ANTHROPIC_API_KEY. This is the only feature that uses a network call. Questions are tailored to the specific content of the submission.
Verification — Alex Rivera
Ask the student these questions in a brief oral or written follow-up:
Question 1 — Comprehension

You mention that AI "plays a crucial role in shaping contemporary discourse." Can you give a specific example from a news story or personal experience that made you think about this?

Question 2 — Reasoning

What does "paradigm shift" mean in this context, and why did you choose that phrase over a simpler term?

Question 3 — Voice

Your essay uses "holistic approach" — what does that mean to you, and where did you first encounter that idea?

Verification questions are a supplementary tool. Student responses should be weighed alongside other evidence before any academic integrity decision.

Export PDF Reports
