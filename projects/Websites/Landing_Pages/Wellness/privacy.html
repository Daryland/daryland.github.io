Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 15, 2026

Overview

Your privacy, plainly stated

Nova Wellness Coaching does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes — ever.

This policy explains what information is collected when you use this website, how it is used, and who it may be shared with. If you have questions, the contact details at the bottom of this page are the right place to start.

Information We Collect

What you provide directly

When you submit an inquiry or book a coaching session, you may provide:

  • Name and email address
  • Health goals or background information shared in intake forms
  • Any other details you voluntarily include in a message

This information is used only to respond to your inquiry, schedule sessions, and deliver coaching services. It is never used for unsolicited marketing.

Payments

Payment processing via Stripe

All payments are processed securely by Stripe. Nova Wellness Coaching does not collect, store, or have access to your credit card number, billing address, or any other payment credentials.

  • Payment data is transmitted directly between your browser and Stripe's servers
  • Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified — the highest level of payment security
  • Stripe's privacy practices are governed by their own policy at stripe.com/privacy
Third-Party Services

Services used on this site

This site uses a small number of third-party services to function:

  • Google Fonts — loads typography assets. Google may log the request. No personal data from this site is shared with Google for advertising.
  • Stripe.js — loads Stripe's payment library. This is required to securely handle any checkout flows.

No advertising networks, tracking pixels, social media embeds, or analytics scripts are loaded on this site.

Data Retention

How long we keep your information

Inquiry and intake information is retained only for as long as necessary to deliver coaching services and fulfill any legal or contractual obligations. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us directly.

Your Rights

Access, correction, and deletion

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

Policy Updates

Changes to this policy
