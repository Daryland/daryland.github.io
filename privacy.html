Privacy Policy for Schedule Rx

Last updated:

Thank you for choosing Schedule Rx. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your information.

The guiding principle of Schedule Rx is simple: Your data belongs to you. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal information, usage data, or metadata.

  • Local Storage Only: All data you enter into Schedule Rx (including vitamin schedules, peptide dosages, amino acid tracking, and logs) is stored locally on your device.
  • No Cloud Syncing: We do not have access to your data. It is not transmitted to any external server or cloud database managed by us.
  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in to use the application.

2. Device Permissions

To function properly, Schedule Rx may request specific permissions on your device. These permissions are used strictly for the app's functionality and do not transmit data to us.

  • Notifications: We may ask for permission to send notifications to remind you of your schedule. These reminders are generated locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services and Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics services. We do not track your behavior within the app, share data with advertising networks, or use software development kits (SDKs) that harvest user data.

4. Data Security and Retention

Because your data is stored offline on your device, the security of your data relies on the security of your device (e.g., your device passcode). If you uninstall the app or lose your device, your data may be lost unless you have backed it up using your operating system’s tools.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
